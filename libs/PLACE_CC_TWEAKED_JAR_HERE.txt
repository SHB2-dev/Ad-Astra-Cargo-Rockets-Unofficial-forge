Place cc-tweaked-1.20.1-forge-api-1.116.0.jar in this folder to enable CC:Tweaked support.

Download from one of:
  - CurseForge: https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/cc-tweaked/files
  - Modrinth:   https://modrinth.com/mod/cc-tweaked/versions

The file you need is the "forge-api" JAR (not the full mod JAR).
After placing the JAR here, run: ./gradlew build
